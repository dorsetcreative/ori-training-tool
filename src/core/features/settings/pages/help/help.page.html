Help/FAQs

I have forgotten my username or password.

If you have forgotten your username or password, please select ‘Forgotten your username or password’ on the home screen where you will be given instructions on how to reset your account details.

How do I gain access to a course?

Locate or search for the desired course (you can click 'All courses...' in the 'My courses' block) and click on the course name. Click Enroll me in this course. Once you are enrolled in a course, it will appear under "My courses" any time that you are logged into the ORI education site.

How do I get back to the home page?

Use the navigation bar at the top left of the page or the button at the very bottom of the course.

How do I jump between my courses?

  1. 'My Courses' block if it has been added to the page you are on
  2. Go back to the homepage and then use the main course block (if it has been added!)

If I need help, who do I contact?

Please contact the team at the Orthopaedic Research Institute, Bournemouth University, if you need any help using the ORI education system ( ori@bournemouth.ac.uk ).

How do I report a technical issue?

Please contact the team at the Orthopaedic Research Institute, Bournemouth University, if you need to report a technical fault with the ORI education system ( ori@bournemouth.ac.uk ).
